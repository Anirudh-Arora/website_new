Climate Data Analysis

Experienced in working with large observational and model datasets, including large ensemble simulations (CESM LENS, CAM6 PPE), single-forcing large ensembles, and multi-model archives (CMIP/DAMIP). Proficient in systematic model evaluation, detection and attribution of climate change, and physically consistent diagnostic frameworks for process-based understanding of regional circulation and precipitation changes.

Numerical Experiments & Model Diagnostics

Developed and customized modeling workflows, including contributions to the SARCI framework for regionally tailored climate simulations. Designed and analyzed targeted numerical experiments to isolate the impacts of external forcings and diagnose structural and parametric sources of uncertainty (e.g., CESM CAM5/CAM6).

High-Performance Computing & Open Science

Experienced in processing and analyzing large climate datasets using CDO, NCO, and shell scripting in Linux/Unix environments, including execution on HPC systems. Committed to reproducible, open-science workflows with version-controlled code and documented pipelines.
