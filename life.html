The Person Behind the Papers

Life Outside the Lab

Research is what I do — but it's not all I am. Here's a glimpse into the things that fuel my curiosity, keep me grounded, and remind me why any of this matters.

🌏
Who I am beyond GCMs
Curious about almost everything.
I grew up in Indore, a city that debates everything from cricket to philosophy over chai at 11pm. That habit of spirited questioning is probably what led me to science — and to debating (I was president of IISER Bhopal's Debating Society).

I find myself equally drawn to questions that have clean equations and those that don't. Climate change is both: the physics is intricate, but the governance, ethics, and communication of it are entirely human problems. I like living in that space between.
🧠
Community
Making Lives Colorful
I coordinate Prayaas, a volunteer organisation at IISER Bhopal working with underprivileged communities through education and social initiatives. Science, I believe, has an obligation to give back.
