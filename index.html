About

Overview

I am a final-year Environmental Science major at IISER Bhopal and a visiting researcher at the DST Centre of Excellence in Climate Modeling, IIT Delhi. My research focuses on atmospheric sciences, general circulation models, and the socio-political dimensions of climate action.

I'm particularly interested in climate dynamics, solar geoengineering,and Indian summer monsoon. I will be joining KAUST in July 2025 for my master's thesis.
